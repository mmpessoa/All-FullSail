Russians find ancient cells in Antarctic. Lifeforms estimated to be 30,000 years old.

March 5, 2012 - SIBERIA

Truth is certainly stranger than fiction. In 1928, horror writer H.P. Lovecraft postulated that ancient lifeforms inhabited the then-unexplored continent of Antarctica. Today, a team of Russian scientists drilling 3 miles down in the ice discovered that the author may have been more right than he knew...
